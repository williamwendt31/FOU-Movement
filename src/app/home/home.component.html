Our Mission

F.O.U Movement. The Gathering of Brothers bringing light to the darkness we once lived in. Restoring Hope to our communities and Empowering each other by Educating the youth against hate and providing resources for addicts and former inmates.

Background

The founders of the organization are Valentino Vaafatu Luafalemana, Ulisese Viliamu, and Peter Afemata. All of whom remain engaged in their communities and seek to provide mentorship and guidance to wayward youth, persons recovering from substance abuse, and former inmates working toward reintegration into society. F.O.U Movement strives to develop and implement community outreach programs to combat hate crimes against perons who identify as Asian American - Pacific Islander. F.O.U movement also plans to develop and establish a network of halfway houses that will facilitate the recovery of persons struggling with substance abuse as well as the reintegration of former inmates while providing resources and support to mitigate recidivism.
